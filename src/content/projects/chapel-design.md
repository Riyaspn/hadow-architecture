A sacred dance of light and form. 

This chapel design reinterprets tradition with a contemporary touch — its folded roof profile creates a dynamic interplay of shadows, while the curvilinear shaft with a rounded base grounds the space in fluid elegance. A sculptural sanctuary where architecture meets divinity.
