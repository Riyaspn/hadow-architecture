Hadow Architecture is an architectural design studio based in Thrissur, Kerala. Our work is characterized by "human-centered" built spaces that emphasize vernacular sensibilities and functional, high-performance environments.

**Design with Intention**
Design isn't just about aesthetics. It's about impact, innovation, and intention. From sketchbooks to site visits — every late night, every bold idea, and every challenge has led to our projects being recognized and shortlisted in platforms like *Inside Architecture Magazine*. We are grateful to be recognized among some of the most inspiring works out there, dreaming big for the future.

**Innovation & Prototyping**
We actively research and prototype the future of construction. From code to concrete, our team witnesses parametric dreams take shape by getting hands-on with technologies like Concrete 3D Printing, exploring how algorithms can revolutionize built forms layer by layer.

**HADOW ZYNC**
We are beyond thrilled to introduce HADOW ZYNC — our next-gen studio app designed to revolutionize how we work, sync, and create. This isn't just a launch. This is HADOW 2.0. A leap into seamless collaboration, live project tracking, smarter client interactions, and a digital studio space that feels just right. Built for visionaries, powered by design.
